# Seasonal Calendar
Last updated: 2025-01-06

## Busy Periods
- **May through September** — summer season, patio open, consistent high revenue
- **December** — holiday parties and group bookings spike revenue 15-20%
- **Valentine's Day week** — strongest single week of Q1
- **Mother's Day weekend** — typically highest single weekend of the year

## Slow Periods
- **January** — slowest month of the year, revenue drops ~30% from December
- **February** — second slowest, slight recovery mid-month around Valentine's Day
- **Early November** — brief lull before holiday season picks up

## Patterns Cortex Has Observed
- Tuesday nights are consistently the weakest night of the week
  averaging 40% less revenue than Friday or Saturday
- Sunday lunch does surprisingly well — often beats Thursday dinner
- Week 1 of every month tends to be slower than weeks 2-3
- Rain noticeably impacts walk-in traffic on weeknights

## Staffing Notes
- Owner tends to over-hire going into Q4 and feels the cost in Q1
- Part-time staff hours are hardest to control during slow periods
- Full crew on Tuesday nights is likely unnecessary based on revenue patterns
