# Stock & Inventory Patterns
Last updated: 2025-01-06

## High Turnover Items (order weekly)
- Pasta (dry) — avg 45lbs/week, spikes to 70lbs in summer
- Crushed tomatoes — avg 30 cans/week
- Fresh produce (mixed) — order twice weekly, spoilage is an issue in summer
- Bread and rolls — daily delivery from local bakery

## Medium Turnover Items (order bi-weekly)
- Olive oil — 8 gallons/two weeks, cost has increased 22% since Jan 2024
- Cheese (mozzarella, parmesan) — 25lbs/two weeks
- Canned goods (various) — stable ordering pattern

## Spoilage Notes
- Fresh produce spoilage runs 8-12% in summer due to volume
- Winter spoilage drops to 3-5% — smaller orders more manageable
- No formal spoilage tracking system currently in place
- Owner estimates $400-600/month lost to spoilage but has not verified

## Supplier Lead Times
- Acosta Foods: 2 day lead time
- Fresh Direct Produce: Next day delivery available
- Local bakery: Same day, order by 8am
