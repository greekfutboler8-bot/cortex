# Anomaly Log
Last updated: 2025-01-06

## 2024-08-15
Food cost hit 32.5% of revenue in August — well above the 28% target.
Revenue was the highest of the year at $86,000 but margins were compressed.
Likely cause: Acosta Foods price increase combined with higher volume purchasing.
Owner was not aware of the margin compression despite the strong revenue month.
Status: Unresolved — menu prices have not been adjusted.

## 2024-09-22
Miscellaneous supplies category jumped from $620/month average to $890 in September.
No purchase record found to explain the increase.
Status: Unresolved — cause unconfirmed.

## 2024-10-01
Three additional part-time staff hired. Monthly part-time labour increased by $1,800.
Labour as a percentage of revenue crossed 33% for the first time.
Owner noted this was intentional for the holiday season but has not set an end date
for the additional hours.
Status: Monitor — labour cost has not come back down.

## 2024-10-15
Miscellaneous supplies remained elevated at $1,240 — double the prior average.
Second consecutive month of unexplained spend in this category.
Status: Unresolved — owner should audit this category.

## 2024-11-01
Labour cost hit 36.1% of revenue in November — highest on record.
Revenue was down slightly while labour costs continued to climb.
Net profit dropped to $13,100 — lowest since February 2024.
Status: Active concern — heading into slow season with high fixed labour costs.

## 2024-12-01
Labour cost slightly improved at 30.7% of revenue due to December revenue spike.
However underlying part-time headcount has not changed.
January will likely expose this cost structure when revenue drops 30%.
Status: Watch — January will be the real test.
