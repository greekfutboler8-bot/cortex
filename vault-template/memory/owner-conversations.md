# Owner Conversations
Last updated: 2025-01-06

## 2024-07-14
Owner mentioned that head chef of 4 years is leaving end of August.
Already interviewing replacements. Concerned about consistency during transition.
Notes: New chef hired September 2024. Transition took about 3 weeks.

## 2024-08-30
Owner said Acosta Foods raised prices again with only a week's notice.
Frustrated but hasn't had time to look for alternatives.
Direct quote: "I'll deal with it after the summer rush."
Notes: Owner has still not renegotiated or found alternative supplier as of January 2025.

## 2024-10-03
Owner decided to hire 3 additional part-time staff for the holiday season.
Reasoning: Last year they were understaffed in December and lost covers.
Notes: No plan discussed for reducing hours after the holiday season ends.

## 2024-11-18
Owner mentioned they are thinking about opening for lunch starting March 2025.
Would add approximately 15-20 hours of operation per week.
Concerned about whether current kitchen staff can handle the additional volume.
No financial analysis done yet on whether lunch service would be profitable.
Notes: Decision not yet made as of January 2025.

## 2024-12-12
Owner said December is going well but is already worried about January.
Direct quote: "Every January I say I'll prepare better and every January 
I'm still scrambling."
Notes: Last two Januaries ended with less than $3,000 cash on hand.

## 2025-01-02
Owner confirmed menu prices have not been updated since March 2024.
Aware that Acosta Foods increases have compressed margins but hasn't 
had time to reprice the menu.
Estimated a 10-12% menu price increase would restore margins to target.
Notes: Owner planning to reprice in February during slow season.
